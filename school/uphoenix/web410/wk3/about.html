All About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs is well-known in the technology community as one of the co-founders of Apple Computer, Inc. and is considered to be one of the leading figures of the technology and entertainment industries.

On this site, you can view Steve's résumé, which includes a detailed history of his work experience. A graphical history of his major product accomplishments can be seen on the portfolio page.
